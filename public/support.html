Quick tips

  • Sign in — use Google or Sign in with Apple (iPhone). Email sign-in is coming later.
  • Join a trip — open the invite link, or paste the join code from Trips.
  • Sync — sign in, then pull to refresh on Trips or open the trip detail.
  • Can’t open a link — make sure you’re on a recent TestFlight / App Store build and signed in.
